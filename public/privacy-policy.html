Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2025

ContextFlow ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication (via Supabase Auth)
  • Page Content: Text from web pages you're viewing, only when you actively use the extension
  • Usage Data: Request counts for enforcing usage limits (Pro: 375/week, Trial: 5 total)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Stripe. We don't store card details.

How We Use Your Data

  • Page content is sent to OpenAI's API to generate AI responses
  • Account data is used for authentication and subscription management
  • Usage counts track your remaining requests

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: API keys and preferences are stored locally in your browser
  • Supabase: Account and subscription data stored securely in our database
  • No Page Content Stored: We don't store the content of pages you visit

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Processes your queries. See OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: Handles authentication and data storage. See Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: Processes payments. See Stripe Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We don't sell your data. We only share data with the third-party services listed above, as necessary to provide the extension's functionality.

Your Rights

  • Delete your account and data by contacting us
  • Export your data upon request
  • Opt out by uninstalling the extension

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and limited data retention.

Children's Privacy

ContextFlow is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated through the extension or email.
